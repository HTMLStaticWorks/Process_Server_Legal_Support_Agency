How We Work.

A clear, transparent workflow ensures your process serving is legally compliant and completed efficiently.

1. Audit & Intake

Documents are reviewed for jurisdictional rules and address accuracy before being cataloged.

2. Locate & Trace

Skip tracing databases are queried to check current employment and address status if needed.

3. Dispatch & Serve

Certified servers execute attempts in the field, cataloging notes and timestamps.

4. Proof & Return

A formal, notarized affidavit of service is generated and returned to the client and court.
